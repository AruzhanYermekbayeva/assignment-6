Skin Types

Normal Skin

Normal skin is well-balanced, not too oily or dry. It's smooth with few imperfections.

Oily Skin

Oily skin is characterized by excess oil production, leading to shine and acne.

Dry Skin

Dry skin feels tight and rough, often flaking and appearing dull.

Sensitive Skin

Sensitive skin is prone to redness and irritation, often reacting to products.

Acne-Prone Skin

Acne-prone skin experiences frequent breakouts, requiring special care.

Combination Skin

Combination skin has both oily and dry areas, typically oily in the T-zone.
